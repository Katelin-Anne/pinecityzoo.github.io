MOS PIZZA

Mos Pizza is a popular spot for delicious, freshly baked pizzas, made with the finest ingredients and cooked to perfection. From classic margherita to meat-lovers, there's something for everyone.

Our pizzas are crafted with love and care, using only the freshest vegetables, meats, and cheeses. Pair your pizza with a side salad or wings for a satisfying meal.

Whether you're grabbing a quick bite or meeting friends for dinner, Mos Pizza is the perfect destination. Enjoy a cozy atmosphere and friendly service.

WHERE WILL YOU FIND IT

You will find Mos Pizza at location F02, in the food court.
