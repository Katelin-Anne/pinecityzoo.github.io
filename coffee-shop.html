COFFEE SHOP

A coffee shop is a cozy spot where people gather to enjoy a wide variety of coffee drinks, from espresso-based beverages to specialty lattes. Many coffee shops also serve teas, pastries, sandwiches, and other light bites.

Coffee shops often serve as community hubs, providing a welcoming atmosphere for friends to meet, work, or simply relax. Some coffee shops feature live music, art exhibits, or book clubs, adding to their charm.

Whether you're looking for a quick caffeine boost or a place to unwind, coffee shops are popular destinations for people of all ages. Many offer outdoor seating, free Wi-Fi, and a relaxed vibe.
