MONKEY TRAIL

The Monkey Trail is an exciting and adventurous path that winds through the trees, mimicking the agile movements of our primate friends. This trail is designed for kids and adults alike to explore and have fun.

Along the Monkey Trail, you'll encounter interactive features like rope bridges, climbing nets, and treehouses. It's a great place to challenge yourself physically and mentally while enjoying the outdoors.

Whether you're a thrill-seeker or just looking for a fun activity, the Monkey Trail is sure to provide an exhilarating experience. Get ready to swing into action!

WHERE WILL YOU FIND IT

You will find the Monkey Trail at location T03, near the adventure zone.
