LOST FOREST

The Lost Forest is a dense and enchanting woodland area, filled with towering trees, twinkling fairy lights, and winding pathways. It's a place where visitors can escape reality and immerse themselves in nature.

As you wander through the forest, you'll discover hidden clearings, sparkling streams, and an array of wildlife, from birds to small mammals. The Lost Forest is a haven for nature lovers, photographers, and anyone seeking tranquility.

With its mystical atmosphere and breathtaking scenery, the Lost Forest is a must-visit destination for anyone looking to connect with the natural world.

WHERE WILL YOU FIND IT

You will find the Lost Forest at location F07, near the nature reserve.
