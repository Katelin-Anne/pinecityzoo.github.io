OUTDOOR RESTAURANT

The Outdoor Restaurant offers a unique dining experience amidst nature, with fresh air, scenic views, and a relaxed atmosphere. Enjoy a wide range of delicious meals, from local cuisine to international flavors.

Savor your meal under the shade of trees or bask in the warm sunshine, depending on your preference. The restaurant's outdoor seating area is perfect for families, couples, and groups of friends.

Whether you're in the mood for breakfast, lunch, or dinner, the Outdoor Restaurant is a great spot to indulge in good food and good company.

WHERE WILL YOU FIND IT

You will find the Outdoor Restaurant at location R01, near the gardens.
